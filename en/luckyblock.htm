Lucky Block BE by weizhijs

Introduction

This Lucky Block Add-on involves Lucky Blocks with various Luck values, Lucky Sword, Lucky Bow, and more!

Please enable "Holiday Creator Features" and "Custom Biomes" in Experimental Gameplay!

Three ways to get Lucky Blocks:

1. By crafting.

2. Good Lucky Blocks(Luck = +75) have a higher probability of being generated in the desert. Bad Lucky Blocks(Luck = -75) are likely to spawn in plains, deserts, etc. Regular Lucky Blocks(Luck = 0) are generated in large numbers on the outer islands of the End dimension, but also, with a small probability, in Overworld dimension or Nether dimension.

3. By using commands. Use
/function luckygive
to get some Lucky Blocks, a Lucky Sword, a Lucky Bow and some Lucky Potions.
Or simply use /give command, like /give @p weizhi:lucky_block
