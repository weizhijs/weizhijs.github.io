Brief introduction

Please enable "Holiday Creator Features" and "Custom Biomes" in Experimental Gameplay!

This Lucky Block Add-on involves Lucky Blocks with various Luck values, Lucky Sword, Lucky Bow, and more!

Three ways to get Lucky Blocks:

1. By crafting.

2. Good Lucky Blocks(Luck = +75) have a higher probability of being generated in the desert. Bad Lucky Blocks(Luck = -75) are likely to spawn in plains, deserts, etc. Regular Lucky Blocks(Luck = 0) are generated in large numbers on the outer islands of the End dimension, but also, with a small probability, in Overworld dimension or Nether dimension.

3. By using commands. Use
/function luckygive
to get some Lucky Blocks, a Lucky Sword, a Lucky Bow and some Lucky Potions.
Or simply use /give command, like /give @p weizhi:lucky_block

The higher the Luck value of the Lucky Block, the greater the probability of hitting something lucky. Items such as iron ingots, gold ingots and diamonds can be used to increase the lucky value. Rotten flesh can be used to reduce the lucky value.

Upgrade recipe: 1 Lucky Block+8 iron ingots/4 gold ingots/2 diamonds. Downgrade recipe: 1 Lucky Block+5 rotten flesh


Lucky Sword, Lucky Bow, and Lucky Potion also have a lot of rich events built in, but be careful that sometimes they can cause damage to you. All three can be obtained by opening a Lucky Block or using /function luckygive . Currently, their lucky value can only be changed by a certain amount of iron ingots or rotten flesh.

Lucky blocks can trigger hundreds of events, items, or combinations. As for what it is, it all depends on luck. Welcome to try!
